DIRECTOR



Dr. Aparna Kakad

Director of MGM Junior College Dr.Aparna Kakkad is a passionate educator and is aligned in spirit, word and action to build an institution with the highest standards of Ethics and Commitment. With a vision of making education a progressive process, she mindfully ensures that every process in the college is child-centric. She has never stopped innovating and infuses the same passion in her team. She seeks opportunities to add value to the community and welcomes their inputs for the college growth and development, thus making the college space for community engagement.

She has pioneered many quality oriented projects and has practical experience in establishing and coordinating Quality Circle activities within her institutions. She has made Presentation at ICQCC(International convention on Quality Circles)competition in Yokohama, Japan in 2011 and Taipei, Taiwan in2013.Both the presentations got the ‘Excellent ‘award. In April 2016, her paper on ‘Sustaining and Institutionalizing Quality Circles ‘was published in the Journal of Quality & Participation (JQP) by the American Society of Quality(ASQ).

A medical graduation and Master in Health Services & Hospital administration from South Bank University London (UK), she has proven her professional ability in Institution Building and people Management. She has participated various training programs in leadership and capability enhancement at Harvard and IIM-A. A strong believer of nurturing culture and enhancing well being of all around her, she has built a team of great educators and administrators. She has often seen digging into books; fishing out gems of knowledge for her staff and students.
